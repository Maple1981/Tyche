# Hellenistic Model

## Core Frame

The chart is read from the Hour-Marker, the rising degree and rising sign. In Whole Sign Houses, the entire rising sign becomes the first place; the next sign in zodiacal order becomes the second place, and the sequence continues until all twelve places are assigned.

The Hour-Marker is the foundation of the chart. Its domicile lord describes the native's life direction, dominant topics, bodily condition, temperament, and the area of life that tends to steer the rest of the nativity. The house containing this lord must be treated as a central interpretive focus.

The system separates signs, planets, and places:

- Signs modify how planets act.
- Planets signify actors, powers, qualities, and events.
- Places assign topics and levels of activity.

The app should not merge sign meanings with house meanings.

## Planets

Base planets are the seven visible wandering stars.

| Planet | Nature | Sect | Core topics |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Sun | Luminary | Day | authority, visibility, judgment, leadership, father, mind, public action |
| Moon | Luminary | Night | body, mother, physical life, household, movement, crowds, fortune |
| Mercury | Common | Variable | speech, writing, calculation, trade, interpretation, youth, messages |
| Venus | Benefic | Night | desire, love, union, adornment, pleasure, friendship, artistic cohesion |
| Mars | Malefic | Night | severing, heat, conflict, injury, courage, force, tools, fire, iron |
| Jupiter | Benefic | Day | justice, abundance, rank, children, alliances, release, trust, honors |
| Saturn | Malefic | Day | cold, restriction, age, isolation, fear, endurance, boundaries, hidden things |

Mercury becomes more diurnal when oriental/morning and more nocturnal when occidental/evening. It is also colored by close contact with benefics or malefics.

## Places

| Place | Name | Condition | Main topics |
|---:|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hour-Marker / Helm | Angular | self, body, life direction, character, vitality |
| 2 | Gate of Hades | Succedent | livelihood, possessions, money, means of living |
| 3 | Goddess | Cadent | siblings, relatives, local movement, messages, ritual, travel |
| 4 | Subterraneous | Angular | home, parents, foundations, hidden matters, endings |
| 5 | Good Fortune | Succedent | children, increase, pleasure, good fortune, gifts |
| 6 | Bad Fortune | Cadent | illness, injury, toil, subordinates, enemies, difficulties |
| 7 | Setting | Angular | partner, marriage, other people, opposition, later life |
| 8 | Idle | Succedent | death, fear, inactivity, other people's resources, decline |
| 9 | God | Cadent | foreign lands, religion, divination, philosophy, astrology |
| 10 | Midheaven | Angular | action, career, reputation, rank, praxis, visibility |
| 11 | Good Spirit | Succedent | friends, allies, hopes, patronage, gifts, acquisition |
| 12 | Bad Spirit | Cadent | enemies, confinement, loss, suffering, forced conditions |

Angular places are strongest, succedent places are moderate or building, and cadent places are declining or less able to act. The sixth and twelfth are especially difficult because they are cadent and in aversion to the Hour-Marker.

## MC and IC in Whole Sign Houses

Whole Sign Houses do not force the MC into the tenth sign. The exact MC can fall in another whole sign place. When it does, it imports tenth-place topics and a strong dynamic quality into that place. The same applies to the IC and fourth-place topics.

The app should show:

- Exact MC degree.
- Whole sign house containing the MC.
- Exact IC degree.
- Whole sign house containing the IC.
