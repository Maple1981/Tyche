# Future Techniques Roadmap

This file defines modules that are deliberately outside Tyche's current static natal judgment. They should not affect the base reading until each module supplies its own inputs, calculations, evidence, and user-facing boundaries.

## Annual Profections

Required input:

- Native's age or target year.
- Birthday convention for charts before the current year's birthday.
- Natal chart already calculated.

Minimum output:

- Profected Lugar/Casa.
- Profected sign.
- Lord of the year.
- Natal house and condition of the lord of the year.
- Activated natal planets, lots, and configurations tied to that profected place.

Guardrail:

- Do not describe dates or activated years from the static natal chart alone.

## Zodiacal Releasing

Required input:

- Lot used as releasing anchor, normally Fortune or Spirit depending on topic.
- Date range or target date.
- Period-lord tables and level structure.

Minimum output:

- Active period and subperiod.
- Releasing from Fortune/Spirit stated explicitly.
- Peak periods, loosing of the bond, and angularity from the releasing lot only after the full module is implemented.

Guardrail:

- Do not blend releasing language into the natal lot reading unless the releasing periods are actually calculated.

## Master of the Nativity

Required input:

- A chosen traditional algorithm, documented before implementation.
- Candidate planets and exclusion rules.

Minimum output:

- Candidate list.
- Selected master.
- Why each candidate was accepted or rejected.

Guardrail:

- Do not identify the Ascendant ruler as Master of the Nativity by default. It may be central, but it is not automatically the same technique.

## Spear-Bearing

Required input:

- Doctrine for the relevant solar/lunar configuration.
- Planet visibility and phase criteria.
- Configurations to the sect light or key significator.

Minimum output:

- Spear-bearing planet or none.
- Relationship to the relevant light/significator.
- Whether the testimony is supportive, protective, or merely present.

Guardrail:

- Do not infer spear-bearing from ordinary angularity or generic aspect contact.

## Fixed Stars

Required input:

- Star catalog with epoch and proper motion handling.
- Rule set: zodiacal conjunction, paran, rising/culminating, or another declared method.
- Orb or visual magnitude threshold.

Minimum output:

- Star name.
- Method by which it contacts the chart.
- Exact distance or timing.
- Significator contacted.

Guardrail:

- Do not add fixed-star text from sign proximity alone without a declared star model.

## Planetary Joys

Required input:

- Joy houses and whether the testimony is treated as descriptive, supportive, or scoring.

Minimum output:

- Planet in its joy, if present.
- House/topic activated.
- Whether this modifies judgment or remains evidence only.

Guardrail:

- Do not let planetary joys compete with domicile, exaltation, sect, angularity, Ascendant lord, or principal lots until the weighting is documented.

## Topic-Specific Readings

Suggested modules:

- Life direction.
- Profession and reputation.
- Relationships.
- Resources.
- Body, illness, and strain.
- Parents, home, and foundations.
- Travel, religion, astrology, and study.

Each topic module needs its own significators and evidence. Do not reuse the general focus score as if it answered every topic.
